  • Smart trolley project

    HTML, CSS, Java

    The idea of a smart trolley is to provide supermarket customers with an easier and improved method of shopping. Each shopping cart will have a barcode scanner and screen installed. Instead of needing to visit the checkout counter customers will be able to scan each item as they add it to their shopping cart. When the customer is done shopping, they can pay directly through the smart screen. This will simplify the shopping process for customers as well as reduce the time spent waiting in the supermarket.
